Ingredients For Mashed Potatoes

  • Potatoes (preferably 3 pounds)
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salt and pepper

Steps To Create The Perfect Mashed Potatoes

  1. First peel and chop the potatoes to large chunks
  2. Secondly, cook the potatoes and make sure they are evenly spaced out
  3. After they are spaced out and cooked, take it out and start to separate any stuck together
  4. Finally, crush the potatoes until they are mashed and easily edible with a fork
